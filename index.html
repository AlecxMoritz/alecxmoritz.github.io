About

I'm Alecx Moritz, I am a full stack JavaScript developer, an avid learner, and passionate about technology, design, art, cats and coffee. I got my start with programming when I was thirteen and I began designing and coding custom game maps. My creative passions tied over into school, where my main studies were art, music, and history. In 2018 I enjolled at the ElevenFifty Academy in an accelerated JavaScript program and graduated three months later with a Core Value Leadership award. My first position after graduating was at that same school as a .NET teacher's assistant, I was shortly after hired on as a JavaScript instructor. I currently teach Angular, React, and serverside JavaScript using Express and Postgres Sequelize, and I am incessantly finding new to build or write.
